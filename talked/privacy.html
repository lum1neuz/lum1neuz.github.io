Talked - Privacy Policy (2023.09.15)

1. Introduction

Welcome to Talked Platform ("Platform"). At Talked Edtech LLC, we are committed to protecting your privacy and safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our Platform. By accessing or using the Platform, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information from users of our Platform:

2.1 Personal Information

Student Information: This includes information such as name, email address, contact information, and payment details when students sign up for our services.

Teacher Information: This includes information such as name, email address, qualifications, and payment details when teachers sign up for our services.

2.2 Usage Information

We collect information about how you use the Platform, such as your browsing history, the pages you visit, the features you use, and the actions you take.

2.3 Technical Information

We automatically collect technical information about your device and internet connection, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifier.

3. How We Use Your Information

We may use your information for the following purposes:

3.1 Providing Services

To connect students and teachers on our Platform.

To facilitate 1-on-1 online education sessions.

To manage accounts.

3.2 Improving Our Services

To analyze usage patterns and improve the functionality of the Platform.

To personalize your experience and provide recommendations.

3.3 Communication

To send you updates, newsletters, and other information about our services.

To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.

3.4 Legal and Security

To comply with legal obligations.

To protect the security and integrity of our Platform.

4. Disclosure of Your Information

We may share your information with third parties under the following circumstances:

With teachers to facilitate education sessions.

With service providers who assist us in operating the Platform.

To comply with legal obligations or in response to a legal request.

In connection with the sale or transfer of our business.

5. Your Choices

You have certain rights and choices regarding your information:

You can access and update your personal information in your account settings.

You can opt out of marketing communications.

You can request the deletion of your account and associated information.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access and disclosure.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the revised policy will be effective immediately.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@talked.mn.
