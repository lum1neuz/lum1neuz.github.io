FAQ

How does Talked work?

Talked tailors your learning journey by matching you with the perfect tutor and creating a personalized course based on your goals.

How to use Talked?

Getting started with Talked is easy!

Download the App: Search for "Talked" in the App Store or Google Play. Once downloaded, register using your phone number and complete your profile information.

Who can become a tutor?

Anyone with a passion and expertise to share can become a Talked tutor! Just apply online, review our comprehensive tutor manuals, and set your flexible schedule through our user-friendly platform.

How to become a tutor?

Simply log in to our app, click the dedicated "Become a Tutor" button, and follow the easy on-screen instructions. We'll guide you through the process!

Can't find the answer you're looking for? We're here to help! Just drop us a line.
