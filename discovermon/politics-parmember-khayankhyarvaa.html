Member of the State Great Khural of Mongolia
Khayankhyarvaa Damdin

Member of the State Great Khural of Mongolia KHAYANKHYARVAA Damdin
Mongolian People's Party

Education, graduated schools, education degree:

1980 Secondary school, Zavhkan aimag
1986 Construction engineer economist, Moscow Institute of Management, USSR

Working experience:

1986 - 1989 Economist, Construction Industry Association, Darkhan city
1989 - 1990 Instructor in Mongolian People’s Revolutionary Party’s committee of Darkhan city
1990 - 1991 Economist, Vice-Chairman of People’s Deputies Hural of Darkhan city
1991 - 1993 Economist, Vice-Director, Darkhan city Iron Plant
1993 - 2000 Head of Department, Governor’s Office, Darkhan-Uul aimag
2000 - 2008 Governor of Darkhan-Uul aimag and Mayor of Darkhan city
2008 - 2012 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia
2010 - 2012 Chairman of the Standing Committee on Budget
2012 Cabinet member and Minister for Finance of Mongolia
2012 - 2016 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia
2016 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia 2016 Leader of MPP caucus

Awards:

1996 Best employee of Economics sector
2002 Best employee of Commercial sector
2003 Order “Polar Star”

Foreign language knowledge:

English; Russian
