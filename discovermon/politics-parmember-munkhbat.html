2011 - 2012 Senior editor, “Mongolyn Unen” daily newspaper
2012 - 2013 Secretary, Mongolian People’s Party, Vice President of Mongolian Social Democratic Youth Union
2013 Secretary General and Board member, Mongolian People’s Party
2016 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia
2016 Cabinet member and Chief of the Cabinet Secretariat
