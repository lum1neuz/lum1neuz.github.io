Member of the State Great Khural of Mongolia
Munkhbaatar Lkhagva

Member of the State Great Khural of Mongolia MUNKHBAATAR Lkhagva
Mongolian People's Party

Education, graduated schools, education degree:

1985 - 1995 Secondary school, Khuvsgul aimag
1995 - 1999 Law school, National University of Mongolia, BA
2006 - 2008 Otgontenger University, LLM University of Humanities, Master degree in Economics
2012 PhD candidate, National University of Mongolia

Working experience:

1999 - 2000 Officer at the Legal Reform Strategy Planning Department, Ministry of Justice
2000 - 2006 Officer, senior officer at the Crime preventing Council, Ministry of Justice and Internal Affairs
2006 - 2008 Director of Information, Monitoring and Evaluation Department, Ministry of Justice and Internal Affairs
2008 - 2009 Head of the National Center for Registration and Information, General Inspector
2009 - 2011 General authority for registration, General registrar
2012 Advisor to the Prime Minister of Mongolia
2013 Advisor to the Head of the Mongolian People’s Party
2014 - 2016 President of the “Khuvsgul Development” foundation
2016 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia

Awards:

2008 Leading employee in legal affairs
2009 Honored Military medal
2011 Order of Independence
2011 Honored Military Order

Foreign language knowledge:

English; Russian
