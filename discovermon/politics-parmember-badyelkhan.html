Member of the State Great Khural of Mongolia
BADYELKHAN Khavdislam

Member of the State Great Khural of Mongolia BADYELKHAN Khavdislam
Mongolian People's Party

Education, graduated schools, education degree:

1971 Secondary School #1, Ulgii soum, Bayan-Ulgii aimag (province)
1976 BA, National University of Mongolia
1990 Political Analyst, the Communist Party Institute, Moscow, Russia
1999 Course at Academy of Management, USA
2002 Course at Academy of Management, Republic of Korea
2004 Course at Academy of Management, Switzerland

Working experience:

1976 - 1978 Engineer, Public bus fleet, Ulaanbaatar
1978 - 1988 Chief Engineer and Director, Transportation Authority of Bayan-Ulgii aimag
1990 - 1991 Head of the State Structure Department of the Citizens' Representatives Hural, Bayan-Ulgii
1991 - 1994 Director, “Urkyen” foreign trade company
1994 - 2000 Director, “Jol” LLC
2000 - 2005 Governor, Bayan-Ulgii aimag
2008 - 2012 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia
2012 - 2014 Advisor, Mongolian People's Party Caucus at the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia
2014 Deputy Minister for Industry
2016 present Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia

Awards:

2004 Order of Labour
2008 Order of Red "Banner of Labour"
2011 Merited Economist of Mongolia

Foreign language knowledge:

English; Russian; German; French
