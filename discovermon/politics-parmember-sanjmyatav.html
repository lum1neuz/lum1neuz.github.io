Vice Chairman of the State Great Khural of Mongolia SANJMYATAV Yadamsuren
Democratic Party

Education, graduated schools, education degree:

1981 Polytechnic in Darkhan city
1989 Zoologist, University of Agriculture
1997 Economist, University of Finance and Economics
1990 Komsomol University, Russia
1998 English course, University of Hydarabad

Working experience:

1981 - 1984 Officer, Supervisor of a group, Erdenet factory
1989 - 1991 Instructor and organizer in charge of provinces at the MRYU
1991 - 1993 Secretary, Executive Committee of the MYU
1993 - 1997 General Secretary of MYU / Chairman of Youth leadership fund
1997 - 1998 Social policy Advisor to the Prime Minister of Mongolia
1998 - 1999 Treasure Secretary, Democratic Party
1999 - 2000 Head of the Government Agency for National Reserve
2003 - 2004 Chairman, “Polar star” Association
2004 - 2008 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia
2007 - 2008 Chairman, Standing Committee on Environment, Food and Agriculture
2004 - 2008 Member of State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia
2007 - 2008 Chairman of the Standing Committee on Nature, food, agriculture of the State Great Hural (Parliament)

2012 - 2016 Member of State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia
2012 - 2014 Minister for Labour
2015 Vice-President of the Mongolian Chess Federation
2016 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia
