Member of the State Great Khural of Mongolia KHURELBAATAR Chimed
Mongolian People's Party

Education, graduated schools, education degree:

1986 Secondary school, Uvs aimag
1991 Economist, Finance and Economics Institute of Leningrad, USSR

Working experience:

1991 - 1992 Scientific researcher, Ministry of National Development
1992 - 1995 Lecturer, School of Economics, National University of Mongolia
1998 - 2000 Senior economist, Economics project
2000 - 2003 Economic Advisor to the President of the Mongolia
2003 - 2007 State Secretary, Ministry of Finance and Economy of Mongolia
2007 - 2008 Cabinet member and Minister for Fuel and Energy of Mongolia
2008 - 2012 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia
2008 - 2009 Chairman of the Standing Committee on Budget
2009 - 2012 Cabinet member and Chief of Cabinet Secretariat
2012 - 2016 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia
2016 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia
2016 Chairman of the Standing Committee on Budget

Awards:

1997 Labour medal
2005 Best employee of the Registration and Statistics sector
2006 Order of “Polar Star”
