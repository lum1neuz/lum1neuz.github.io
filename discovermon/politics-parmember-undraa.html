Member of the State Great Khural of Mongolia
Undraa Agvaanluvsan

Member of the State Great Khural of Mongolia UNDRAA Agvaanluvsan
Mongolian People's Party

Education, graduated schools, education degree:

1989 - 1994 BA in Physics, National University of Mongolia
1994 - 1995 MA in Theoretical physics, National University of Mongolia
1996 - 1997 Diploma in High Energy Physics, International Centre for Theoretical Physics, Trieste, Italy
1998 - 2002 Ph.D. in Physics, North Carolina State University, Raleigh, USA

Working experience:

1997 - 1998 Lecturer of physics, National University of Mongolia
2002 - 2004 Postdoctoral staff member and research scientist at North Carolina State University and Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory, USA
2008 - 2010 Professor (visiting) and Science Fellow at Stanford University, USA
2009 - 2010 Secretary, Institute of Strategic Studies, National Security Council of Mongolia
2010 - 2011 Ambassador-at-large, Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Mongolia
2008 - 2012 Adjunct Professor of Physics, North Carolina State University, USA Directed and co-advised PhD level work for 4 graduate students
2012 Advisor to the Minister of Mineral Resources and Energy of Mongolia
2014 - 2016 Director, The Strategy Academy of MPRP
2016 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia

Awards:

2011 Order of Labour

Foreign language knowledge:

English; Russian
