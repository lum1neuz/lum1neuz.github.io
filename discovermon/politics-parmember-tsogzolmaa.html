Member of the State Great Khural of Mongolia
Tsogzolmaa Tsedenbal

Member of the State Great Khural of Mongolia TSOGZOLMAA Tsedenbal
Mongolian People's Party

Education, graduated schools, education degree:

1986 - 1992 Irkutsk State University, Russia
2000 - 2004 Academy of Management, Mongolia
2005 - 2009 BA in law, Intelligence Academy, Mongolia

Working experience:

1992 - 1993 Lecturer, National University of Mongolia in Khovd aimag
1993 - 1994 Specialist, Governor’s office of Khovd aimag
1994 - 1995 Specialist, Citizens’ Representatives Hural of Khovd aimag
1995 - 1996 Specialist, Children’s Centre of Khovd aimag
1996 - 1997 Cahirmowan, Women’s Council of Khovd aimag
1997 - 1998 Program director, Mongolian Scout's Association
1998 - 1999 Chief group leader, “Nairamdal” International children’s center
1999 - 2000 Chairperson, Democratic Socialist Youth Association of Ulaanbaatar
2000 - 2004 Head of Children and Youth Development Authority of Ulaanbaatar
2004 - 2006 Governor of Sukhbaatar district
2006 - 2008 Head of office, National Committee on Gender Equality
2008 - 2012 Deputy Mayor of Ulaanbaatar in charge of Social Development
2013 President of Social Democracy - Mongolian Women’s Association
2016 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia

Awards:

2004 Order of Labour
2010 Order of “Polar Star”

Foreign language knowledge:

English, Russian
