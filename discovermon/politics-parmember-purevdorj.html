Purevdorj Bukhchuluun

Member of the State Great Khural of Mongolia PUREVDORJ Bukhchuluun
Democratic Party

Education, graduated schools, education degree:

1980 - 1988 Middle school, Khovd aimag
1988 - 1990 Secondary school of Mankhan soum, Khovd aimag
1990 - 1994 BA in English teaching and International relations, National University of Mongolia

Working experience:

1995 - 2008 Customs Inspector, Head of branches in Bayan-Ulgii, Buyant-Ukhaa and Ulaanbaatar city
2008 - 2012 Executive Director, “Western Mongolian International LLC"
2009 - 2012 Head of Democratic Party in Khovd aimag
2005 - 2012 Member of National Council of Democratic Party
2011 - 2012 Member of Executive Committee of Democratic Party
2012 - 2014 Head, Authority of Foreigns and immigration of Mongolia
2014 - 2015 Head of Immigration of Mongolia
2015 Chairman, “Khovddoo saikhan amidartsgaaya” fund
2016 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia

Awards:

2009 Order of “Polar Star”

Foreign language knowledge:

English; Russian
