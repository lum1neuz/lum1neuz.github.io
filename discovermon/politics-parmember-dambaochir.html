Member of the State Great Khural of Mongolia
Damba-ochir Dorjdamba

Member of the State Great Khural of Mongolia DAMBA-OCHIR Dorjdamba
Mongolian People' Party

Education, graduated schools, education degree:

1971 - 1981 Secondary school #1, Selenge aimag
1981 - 1986 BA in Automatic engineering, National Polytechnic University in Odessa, Russia
2002 Doctor of Technical science, National University of Science and Technology (MISIS) in Moscow, Russia

Working experience:

1991 - 2000 Founder, “Ochir Tuv” LLC
2000 - 2005 Sales director, Erdenet Mining Corporation
2005 - 2008 Chairman, Mongolian People's Party in Erdenet city
2008 - 2012 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia
2013 - 2014 Chairman, Board of Directors, “Ochir Tuv Holding” LLC
2014 - 2015 Advisor to the Deputy Minister, Cabinet Secretary, Government of Mongolia
2015 - 2016 Chairman, Board of Directors, “Ochir Tuv Holding” LLC
2016 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia

Foreign language knowledge:

English; Russian
