We will supply you with all necessary information in accordance with your flight.

Company History

Our company was established in 1997 with a 100% investment as the first International Airline ticket We have always been the leader in this industry; being the foremost company to be permitted to issue tickets from the IATA (International Air Transport Association) and to have initially introduced the GALILEO airline ticket booking system. Air Trans is the official agency in Mongolia of not only MIAT, Air China, Aeroflot and Korean Air airlines that start from Ulaanbaatar but also of the following airlines: United Airlines, All Nippon Airways, Singapore Airlines, Lufthansa, Thai Airways and Air Astana

It is our main purpose to provide our customers with issuing reliable and reasonable International airline tickets from a wide range of choices.

We work towards giving you assistance on choosing a healthy, untiring and the shortest flight, and providing you with all necessary information and service.

The development of any country is directly correlated to its airline industry; thus, we see it an honor to be offering our contribution to this industry.
