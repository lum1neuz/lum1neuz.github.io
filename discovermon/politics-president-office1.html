Chief of Staff of the President of Mongolia Mr. BAYARSAIKHAN Tsevelmaa

 MR. Tsevelmaa BAYARSAIKHAN was born in Umnugovi Aimag, in 1962. In 1980, he was graduated from the primary school of Umnugovi Aimag, in 1986, the University of Economics of Moscow, the former Soviet Union, as engineer- economist, and in 1989 he was graduated from the National University of Mongolia as economist, respectively.

Working experience

1986 – 1989,  Specialists at the Administration of the Meeting of People's Deputies of Umnugovi Aimag
1989 – 1990,  Senior specialist at the Economic Planning Committee of Umnugovi Aimag
In 1990,  Head of Administration of the Meeting of People's Deputies of Umnugovi Aimag
1990 – 1992,  First Deputy Chairman of the Administration of the Meeting of People's Deputies of Umnugovi Aimag
1992 – 1996,  Deputy of the Governor of Umnugovi Aimag
1996 – 2000,  Member of the Parliament of Mongolia
1996 – 1998,  Chairman of the Parliamentary Standing Committee on Environment and Rural Development
2001 – 2004,  Teacher of the Institute of Finance and Economics
2004 – 2008,  Member of the Parliament of Mongolia
2004 – 2006,  Member of the Government of Mongolia, Minister of Social Welfare and Labour
2008 – 2012,  Member of the Parliament of Mongolia
2008 – 2011,  Chairman of the Parliamentary Standing Committee on Economics
2012 – 2016,  Member of the Parliament of Mongolia
2012 – 2014,  Member of the Government of Mongolia, Minister of Construction and Urban Development
2015 – 2016,  Chairman of the Parliamentary Standing Committee on Economics
Mr. Ts.Bayarsaikhan was elected as the Member of the Parliament four times and was appointed as the Chief of Staff of the President of Mongolia on August, 2016.

Awards

In 1993,  the Outstanding Worker of Registration and Statistics
In 1997,  the Outstanding Worker of Food and Agriculture sector
In 2006,  the Order of Polar Star
In 2010,  the Order of Labour Merit
In 2016,  the State Merit Economist
