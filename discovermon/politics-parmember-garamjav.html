Member of the State Great Khural of Mongolia
Garamjav Tseden

Member of the State Great Khural of Mongolia GARAMJAV Tseden
Mongolian People's Party

Education, graduated schools, education degree:

1969 Secondary school #4, Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia
1975 Secondary school #33, Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia
1981 BSc in Geological Engineering, National University of Mongolia
1984 Geological Engineering Certificate, Soil Research Program, Moscow State University, Russia
1994 Legal specialist's certificate, International regulation and contracts in the mineral resources sectors, Rome, Italy

Working experience:

1983 - 1989 Engineer, Construction engineering drawing institute, Engineer, Senior engineer, laboratory manager at Geological department
1989 - 1992 Researcher, Engineering Exploration Department, Construction Engineering Drawing Institute, Mongolia
1992 - 2012 General Director and Founder of Monpolymet Ltd
2012 Deputy Minister for Mineral Resources and Energy of Mongolia
2012 - 2016 Advisor to Monpolymet Group
2016 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia
2016 Chairwoman of the Standing Committee on Nature, Environment, Food and Agriculture

Awards:

2006 Order of “Polar Star”

Foreign language knowledge:

English; Russian
