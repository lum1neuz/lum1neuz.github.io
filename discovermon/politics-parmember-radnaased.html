Radnaased Shatarbal

Member of the State Great Khural of Mongolia RADNAASED Shatarbal
Mongolian People's Party

Education, graduated schools, education degree:

1998 BA in law, School of law, National University of Mongolia

Working experience:

1998 - 2000 Specialist, Strategy management, integrated planning of Legal Reform Department, Ministry of Justice
2000 - 2007 Senior officer, Legal department, Cabinet secretariat
2007 - 2009 Head, Anti-Corruption prevention and awareness department
2009 Legal policy advisor to the President of Mongolia
2010 - 2014 Advisor to Secretary General of MPP, Head of Secretariat, MPP, Head of the Party Development Department
2014 - 2015 Head of office, MPP caucus in the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia
2015 - 2016 Head, Special Inspection Agency of Mongolia
2016 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia
2016 Chairman of Standing Committee on Legal Affairs

Awards:

2009 Order of “Polar Star”

Foreign language knowledge:

English; Russian
