Member of the State Great Khural of Mongolia
Sarangerel Davaajantsan

Member of the State Great Khural of Mongolia SARANGEREL Davaajantsan
Mongolian People's Party

Education, graduated schools, education degree:

1979 Secondary school, Ulaanbaatar
1983 Photographer specialist, Technical school of Omsk, USSR
1990 Journalist, University of Rostov, USSR

Working experience:

1982 - 1985 Laboratory technician, Color Photography Department, Mongolian National Committee on Radio and Television
1990 - 1991 Journalist and editor at the Mongolian National Television
1991 - 1992 Senior editor, Mongolian National Television
1993 - 1994 Editor in chief, Mongolian National Television
1995 - 1996 Director, MM agency
1999 - 2000 Director, MM agency
1997 - 1999 Editor in chief, MM agency
2000 - 2003 Editor, General Studio for Social and Economic Affairs
2003 - 2005 General director, TV5 television
2005 - 2011 President of the Mongolian Journalists’ Union
2011 - 2012 Secretary, Mongolian People’s Party
2012 - 2016 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia
2016 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia
2016 Chairwoman of the Standing Committee on Petitions

Awards:

2002 Order of “Polar Star”
2009 Merited employee of Culture

Foreign language knowledge:

English; Russian
