Member of the State Great Khural of Mongolia
Terbishdagva Dendev

Member of the State Great Khural of Mongolia TERBISHDAGVA Dendev
Mongolian People's Party

Education, graduated schools, education degree:

1974 Secondary school-12, Ulaanbaatar city
1982 Food engineer and technologist, Humboldt University, Democratic Republic of Germany

Working experience:

1982 - 1983 Engineer, Ulaanbaatar meat processing factory
1983 - 1984 Technologist, Ulaanbaatar meat processing factory
1984 - 1986 General technologist, Ulaanbaatar meat processing factory
1986 - 1988 Director, Ulaanbaatar meat processing factory
1988 - 1990 Interpreter and lecturer, Free Youth Institute of Germany
1992 - 1994 Senior officer, Bernau city office, Germany
1994 - 2000 General director, “TBD Anduud” LLC
2000 - 2002 Deputy Minister for Ministry of Food and Agriculture of Mongolia
2002 - 2004 Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary Ambassador of Mongolia to the Federal Republic of Germany
2004 - 2008 Member of State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia
2004 - 2007 Cabinet member and Minister for Food and Agriculture of Mongolia
2008 - 2012 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia
2012 - 2014 Cabinet member and Deputy Prime Minister of Mongolia
2012 - 2016 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia
2016 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia
2016 Chairman of the Standing Committee on Economic Affairs

Awards:

2006 Order of “Polar Star”
2009 Order of "Red Banner of Labour"

Foreign language knowledge:

English; Russian; German
