Education, graduated schools, education degree:

1995 - 1999 BA in Economics, “Dokuz Eylul” University of Turkey
2004 - 2006 MA in International Development Economics, National University of Seoul, Korea and International University of Japan
2007 - 2014 Post Graduate program at the School of Economics and Business, Mongolian University of Agriculture Ph.D. in Economics and Management

2012  Governor, Chingeltei District of Ulaanbaatar City
2012  Chairman, Policy Council of the Reform Committee of the MPP
2015  Chairman, Fair Competition and Consumer Protection Agency, the Government Regulatory body
2016  Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia

Awards:

Foreign language knowledge:
