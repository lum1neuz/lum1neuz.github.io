Centre of aimag-Mandalgobi town, located 265 km away from UB.
Number of somons-16
Dundgobi aimag was established in 1942
Territory-74.7 thousand km2
Livestock- 2 109.6 thousand
Governor BATERDENE.O

The Dundgobi aimag is semi-desert and steppe. The average elevation of the area is 990-1926 meter above sea level and the highest peak is Delgerkhangai peak in the Delgerkhangai somon. There are semi-desert, such as Oldokh, Tugrug, Nomgon, Ergenegene, and springs, such as Togoo, Ungut, Khadan Us, This aimag is full of beautiful places such as Ikhe Gazriin Chuluu, Baga Gazriin Chuluu, Sum khukh oasis, Tsagaan stupa-piramid, Uush dunes to attract tourists. The elm, saksaul, tamarisk, almond and are the woody plants, and medical herbs, such as attemesia, mongol onion, psammochlaa, splenden, feather-grass, wornwood, couch, wild-rose, liquirice, cynomorium, agriophyllum, annua, cotoneatter grow in the aimag. Rare animals are wild sheep, ibex, antilope, black tailed antilope, marmot, fox, lynx, corsa, wild cat, badger and hare. Birds are eagle, kite, swan, ottis tarda, pallous and sandgrouse. They inhabit this area. The Dundgovi aimag is rich in coal, copper ore, iron ore, fluorspar, nephrite, jade, graphite, marble and chalcedony. The main branch of economy is the animal husbandry. The number of livestock is 1.780 thousand heads. The aimag is rich in natural resources and has several discovered deposits on its territory.
