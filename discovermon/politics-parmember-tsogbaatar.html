Member of the State Great Khural of Mongolia
Tsogbaatar Damdin

Member of the State Great Khural of Mongolia TSOGBAATAR Damdin
Mongolian People's Party

Education, graduated schools, education degree:

1988 Secondary school #14, Ulaanbaatar city
1988 - 1994 Political analyst, Moscow State Institute of International Relations
1998 LLM, Australian National University

Working experience:

1994 - 1996 Attache, Asia and Africa Department, Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Mongolia
1998 - 2000 Officer, Foreign Trade and Economic Cooperation Department
2000 - 2002 Deputy director, Multilateral Cooperation Department
2002 - 2008 Foreign Policy Advisor to the President of Mongolia
2008 - 2011 State Secretary, Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Mongolia
2011 - 2012 Foreign Policy Advisor to the Prime Minister of Mongolia
2012 - 2014 Cabinet member and Minister for Nature, Environment and Tourism of Mongolia
2014 - 2015 Cabinet member and Minister for Construction and Urban Development of Mongolia
2015 Chairman, MPRP Committee of Ulaanbaatar
2016 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia

Awards:

2006 Order of “Polar Star”
2009 Order of "Red Banner of Labour"

Foreign language knowledge:

English, Russian, Khmer, Thai
