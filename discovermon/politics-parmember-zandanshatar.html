Member of the State Great Khural of Mongolia
Zandanshatar Gombojav

Member of the State Great Khural of Mongolia ZANDANSHATAR Gombojav
Mongolian People's Party

Education, graduated schools, education degree:

1987 High school #77, Ulaanbaatar city
1992 BA in Economics and financing, State Agriculture Institute, Russia
2004 MA at Maastricht University, Netherlands

Working experience:

1991 - 1992 Researcher, Business and Trade College
1992 - 1995 Lecturer in Finance and Economics, College of Market Studies
1995 - 1998 Economist, Manager, Head of department, Agriculture Bank
1998 Manager of the Training Center, Mongol Bank
1998 - 2000 Member of the Representatives Council of the Mongol Bank to the Agriculture Bank
2000 - 2003 Deputy director, Khan Bank
2003 - 2004 Deputy Minister for Food and Agriculture of Mongolia
2004 - 2012 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia
2008 - 2012 Minister for Foreign Affairs and Trade of Mongolia
2012 - 2013 Secretary General, Mongolian People's Party
2014 - 2016 Senior consultant, Center for Deliberative Democracy, Stanford University
2016 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia

Awards:

2009 Order of “Polar Star”

Foreign language knowledge:

English; Russian
