Chairman of the State Great Khural of Mongolia
Enkhbold Miyegombo

Chairman of the State Great Khural ENKHBOLD Miyegombo
Mongolian People's Party

Education, graduated schools, education degree:

1982  Secondary school #1, Ulaanbaatar city
1987  Economist, National University of Mongolia
1996 Advanced course in Public administration, Japan
1998 Advanced course in Local Governance, Germany
2000 MA in Economics, National University of Mongolia

Working experience:

1987 - 1989 Economist, General Public Service Authority of Ulaanbaatar
1989 - 1991 Officer, Planning and Regulatory Department, Ministry of General Public Service
1991 - 1992 Head of General Service Department, Executive Administration of the People’s Deputies Hural
1992 - 1996 Deputy Governor, Chingeltei district, Ulaanbaatar
1996 - 1998 Chairman, Citizens Representatives Hural, Chingeltei district, Ulaanbaatar
1997 - 2005 Chairman, Capital city MPRP branch
1999 - 2005 Governor of the Capital city and Mayor of Ulaanbaatar
2005 - 2007 Chairman, Mongolian People’s Revolutionary Party
2005 - 2008 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia
2006 - 2007 Prime Minister of Mongolia
2008 - 2012 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia
2007 - 2012 Deputy Prime Minister of Mongolia
2012 - 2016 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia
2012 - 2016 Vice-Chairman of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia
2013 Chairman of Mongolian People’s Party
2016 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia
2016 Chairman of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia

Awards:

1999  Order “Polar Star”
2005  Order of Red Banner of Labour
2005  Honourable citizen of the Capital city
