Member of the State Great Khural of Mongolia
Ganbold Davaa

Member of the State Great Khural of Mongolia GANBOLD Davaa
Mongolian People's Party

Education, graduated schools, education degree:

1972 - 1982 Secondary School #72, Ulaanbaatar City
1983 - 1987 Industrial Accountant and Economist, University of Tyumen City, Russia
1995 - 1997 Public Administration Manager, Public Administration and Management Institute
1994 - 1998 Economist and Lawyer, National University of Mongolia

Working experience:

1987 - 1988 Supervisor, Accountant at the Financial Department, Executive Administration Office of People's Deputies Hural of Nalaikh District of Ulaanbaatar
1988 - 1989 Frontier soldier, Border Troop Unit in Dornod aimag
1989 - 1990 Chief Accountant at the Financial Department, Executive Administration Office of People's Deputies Hural of Sukhbaatar District of Ulaanbaatar
1990 - 1992 Inspector for Taxation and Financial Monitoring at the Financial Department, Executive Administration Office of the Ulaanbaatar
1992 - 2000 Head of the Financial Department, the Governor's Office of Chingeltei District Ulaanbaatar
2000 - 2008 Head of the Financial and State Treasure Department, the Ulaanbaatar City Mayor's office
2008 - 2016 Governor of the Chingeltei District
2016 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia

Awards:

2005 Order of Labour
2010 Order of “Polar Star”

Foreign language knowledge:

English, Russian
