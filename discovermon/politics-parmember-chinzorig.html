Member of the State Great Khural of Mongolia
Chinzorig Sodnom

Member of the State Great Khural of Mongolia CHINZORIG Sodnom
Mongolian People's Party

Education, graduated schools, education degree:

1982 Secondary school Khujirt sum, Uvurkhangai aimag
1986 BA in Economics, National University of Mongolia

Working experience:

1986 - 1988 Officer, Planning commission, Executive administration of People's Deputies Hural of Uvurkhangai aimag
1990 - 1992 Head of department, Executive administration of the People's Deputies Hural of Uvurkhangai aimag
1992 - 1996 Deputy Aimag Governor in charge of Finance and Economics
1996 - 2000 Head, Aimag governor's office
2000 - 2008 Deputy Minister for Social Welfare and Labor of Mongolia
2008 - 2010 Deputy Chief, President’s Office
2010 - 2012 Advisor on Social Welfare to the Prime Minister of Mongolia
2013 Head of department, MPRP
2014 - 2015 Cabinet member and Minister for Labour of Mongolia
2016 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia

Awards:

2001 Order of “Polar Star”
2009 Order of "Red Banner of Labour"
