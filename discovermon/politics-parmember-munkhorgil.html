Member of the State Great Khural of Mongolia
Munkh-Orgil Tsend

Member of the State Great Khural of Mongolia MUNKH-ORGIL Tsend
Mongolian People's Party

Education, graduated schools, education degree:

1982 Secondary school #23
1988 BA at Moscow State Institute of International Relations (MGIMO)
1996 LLM at Harvard Law School

Working experience:

1988 - 1991 Attache, Ministry of Foreign Affairs
1991 - 1995 Third secretary, Permanent Mission of Mongolia to the UN
1996 - 1997 Lawyer at a Washington international law firm
1997 - 2000 General Director, "Munkh-Orgil. Idesh, Lynch" Law firm
2000 - 2004 Deputy Minister for Justice and Internal Affairs of Mongolia
2004 - 2008 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia
2004 - 2006 Minister for Foreign Affairs of Mongolia
2006 - 2007 Chairman of the Standing Committee on Legal affairs
2007 - 2008 Minister for Justice and Internal Affairs of Mongolia
2012 - 2013 Secretary, Mongolian People’s Party
2016 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia
2016 Cabinet member and Minister for Foreign Affairs of Mongolia

Awards:

Order of “Polar Star”
Order of "Red Banner of Labour"

Foreign language knowledge:

English; Russian; French
