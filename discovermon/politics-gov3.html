Prime Minister of Mongolia
ERDENEBAT Jargaltulga

Education, graduated schools, education degree:

1991  Secondary school, Selenge aimag
1995  Economist, Trade and Industry Institute
1998  Mongolian Professional Accountants’ Institute
2004  MA in Economics, Academy of Administration
2005  Mongolian National University of Agriculture

Working experience:

1996 - 1997  Accountant at the “Suutei” LLC
1997 - 2000  Officer at the Inspection Commission
2000 - 2004  Head of the Budget and financial policy adjustment department, Secretariat of the Governor’s office at the Selenge province
2004 - 2005  Deputy Governor of Selenge province
2005 - 2008  Head of the Budget and state treasury department, Secretariat of the Governor’s office at the Selenge province
2008 - 2012  Governor of Selenge province
2012 - Present  Member of State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia
2016 - Present  The Prime Minister of Mongolia

Awards:

2005  Best employee of the banking and financial sector
2005  Best employee of the sports sector
