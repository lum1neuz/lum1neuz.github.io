Aero Mongolia

Address: Buyant-Ukhaa Airport
Ulaanbaatar-34, Mongolia
Tel: +976-11-330373
Fax: +976-11-330374
Web: http://www.aeromongolia.mn

The 3rd of June 2003 will be remembered as a land-mark in the history of Mongolian Aviation. It was a day when the private airline entered the nation’s skies with the commitment to serve regular and charter fligths to the travelling public.

  • Aero Mongolia was founded in 2001
  • Two international destinations ( Irkuts in Russia and Huh Hot in China)
  • Five domestic destinations ( Choibalsan, Hovd, Ulaangom, Murun and Bayankhongor)
  • Charter flights to Umnu-Gobi “Juulchin” tourist camp and Oyu-Tolgoi.

Our pilots are highly experienced. Most of them have been flying with the MIAT Mongolian Airlines at distances between 8000-23000 kilometers. They have all been trained in the prestigious SAS Flight Academy in Sweden.

Our specialist engineers and technicians have been involved in the training at the Fokker Service Centre in Holland, improving their theoretical and practical knowledge.

We associate with well known international companies such as Pratt & Whitney, AAR Component Services, Fokker Services and SAS Flight Academy that specialise in the maintenance and supply of aircrafts and its components.

We intend to provide the most comprehensive customer service to the best of our ability. We thank you for the your attention and are always happy to serve you.
