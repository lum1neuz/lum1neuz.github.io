Saranchimeg Batsukh

Member of the State Great Khural of Mongolia SARANCHIMEG Batsukh
Mongolian People's Party

Education, graduated schools, education degree:

1993 BA in Engineering, Novosibirsk Electrical Technical University, Russian Federation
2013 MA in computer engineering

Working experience:

1994 - 1999 System administrator, Secretariat of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia
2006 - 2009 Chairwoman, “New Century UB” NGO
2010 - 2015 Director, city enterprise the “National Park”
2011 Head, Mongolian Social Democratic Women's Association of Ulaanbaatar
2015 Advisor to the Minister of Environment, Green Development and Tourism of Mongolia
2016 Member of the State Great Hural (Parliament) of Mongolia

Awards:

2012 Order of "Polar Star"
2015 Order of "Red Banner of Labour"

Foreign language knowledge:

English; Russian
